Our Health Services

At SGMP, we provide both curative and preventive health services with the help of our genuine and experienced healthcare providers. Our team is dedicated to ensuring the well-being of our community through quality healthcare solutions.

The western part of Karnali Province faces numerous health-related challenges, making it one of the most underserved regions in Nepal. As a backward province, it lacks adequate healthcare infrastructure and resources. We are deeply committed to working in such areas to improve health outcomes and provide essential services to those in need.

You can actively guide us in our programs by sharing your insights and experiences. Alternatively, you can benefit from our services, including counseling and medical consultations provided by our highly skilled and compassionate doctors. Together, we can make a meaningful impact and bring positive change to the lives of the people in Karnali Province.
